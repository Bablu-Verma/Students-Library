Refund Policy Overview

At Students Library, we understand that circumstances may change. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are provided for membership fees. We aim to be fair and transparent in all our refund processes.

Note: We offer a 2-day FREE trial for all new members. We recommend using the trial period to ensure our library meets your needs before purchasing a membership.

Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

Time Period Refund Amount Status
Within 3 Days
90% Refund ✓ Eligible
Within 7 Days
70% Refund ✓ Eligible
Within 15 Days
50% Refund ✓ Eligible
After 15 Days
No Refund ✗ Not Eligible

Example: If you paid ₹899 for a Two-Shift membership and request a refund within 7 days, you will receive ₹629 (70% of ₹899).

Non-Refundable Items

The following fees and charges are non-refundable under any circumstances:

Locker Charges

₹100/month locker rental fee is non-refundable

Priority Seating

₹200/month priority seat booking fee is non-refundable

Registration Fee

One-time registration/ID card fee (if any)

Promotional Offers

Discounted memberships or special offers are non-refundable

How to Request a Refund

Follow these simple steps to request a refund:

1

Contact Us

Reach out to us via WhatsApp at +91 8287678088 or visit the library in person.

2

Submit Request

Provide your membership ID, reason for refund, and payment receipt (if available).

3

Verification

Our team will verify your membership details and refund eligibility within 24-48 hours.

4

Receive Refund

Once approved, refund will be processed within 5-7 business days via the original payment method.

Membership Transfer (Alternative to Refund)

Instead of a refund, you may transfer your remaining membership period to another person:

Transfer Benefits

  • No deduction - Full remaining period transferred
  • Available anytime during your membership
  • One-time transfer allowed per membership
  • New member must complete registration with valid ID

Transfer Fee: A nominal transfer fee of ₹50 will be charged for processing the membership transfer.

Special Circumstances

We understand that unexpected situations may arise. Full or partial refunds may be considered in the following special cases:

Medical Emergency

Serious illness or hospitalization (medical certificate required)

Job Relocation

Transfer to another city (offer letter/proof required)

Family Emergency

Urgent family matters requiring relocation (case-by-case basis)

Library Closure

If library closes permanently, pro-rata refunds will be provided

Special circumstance refunds are reviewed individually. Please contact us with supporting documents for consideration.

When Refunds Are NOT Provided

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

  • Request made after 15 days of membership purchase
  • Membership terminated due to rule violations
  • Unused days due to personal reasons (vacation, exams, etc.)
  • Change of mind after using the library
  • Discounted or promotional memberships
  • Temporary service disruptions (power cuts, maintenance, etc.)
